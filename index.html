MYvalues

What is MYvalues?

MYvalues is, in essence, a political quiz. You will be presented by a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be compared to the maximum possible for each value, thus giving you a percentage. Answer honestly!

There are questions in the test.

What are the eight values?

There are four independent axes - Economic, Wack, Basedness, and Gamer - and each has two opposing values assigned to them. They are:

EQUALITY

Those with higher Equality scores believe the economy should distribute value evenly among the populace. They tend to support progressive tax codes, social programs, and at high values, socialism.

ECONOMIC

MARKETS

Those with higher Market scores believe the economy should be focused on rapid growth. They tend to support lower taxes, privatization, deregulation, and at high values, laissez-faire capitalism.

WACKY

Those with higher Wacky scores have ideologies that deviate from the status quo. Usually wacky ideologies are against liberal values, Capitalism, Democracy, or some combination of thereof

WACK

NORMAL

Those with higher Normal scores have ideologies that stay close to the status quo. These ideologies will be seen often represented by mainstream parties in liberal democracies

NORMIE

Those with higher Normie scores have an ideology which is undeniably cringe. These ideologies are just not cool.

BASEDNESS

BASED

Those with higher Based scores have an ideology that is very epic and redpilled. Having a based ideology makes you a cool person automatically.

GAMER

Those with higher Gamer scores have ideologies that commit acts which can be described as epic gamer moments. whether those actions are good or not is up for debate.

GAMER

BORING

Those with higher Boring scores have ideologies that do not commit acts of epic gamer status.


What's the "Closest Match" mean at the bottom of the results?

In addition to matching you to the eight values, the quiz also attempts to match you to a political ideology. If you disagree with your assigned ideology, you should shut the fuck up and rethink your life choices. Thanks!
